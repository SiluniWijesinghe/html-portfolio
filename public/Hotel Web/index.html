Offering a convenience shop and a lobby area, the 3-star Hotel Transylvania New York lies in Manhattan district, a 25-minute stroll of Broadway Road and within 10 minutes' ride from Bowery Street. There is a dry cleaning and valet parking at the hotel.

Steps away from the multi-purpose indoor arena "Madison Square Garden", this pet friendly accommodation is located in the heart of New York. The hotel is placed in a 10-minute drive from The High Line Public Park, while Penn is just 200 metres away.

There is a 1705 units equipped with an ironing set and air-conditioning along with modern facilities including high-velocity internet. Bathrooms with a bathtub and a separate toilet, also offer a hair dryer and bath sheets.

You can enjoy American cuisine in Andrew's Coffee Shop, situated close at hand, only 250 metres away. LaGuardia airport is a 17-minute drive away, while New York Penn railway station is within 10 minutes' walk. Corporate travellers will benefit from meeting rooms available on site.

A Tradition Of Excellence

The premier private sector international educational institute in Sri Lanka, Cloud Institute International School in Sri Lanka is recognized exclusively for its unblemished reputation in producing charismatic global citizens resplendent with knowledge, skills and humanity. Its far-reaching roots date back to 1971, when the Founder Chairman, Mr. G. T. Bandara, laid the strong foundation to a ‘home away from home’, where hundreds and thousands of youngsters found love, care and warmth to an extent more than their parents could have ever envisaged. With the gentle but sturdy growth of the learners, grew the institution, spreading its mighty wings to serve and shield thousands of more youngsters of tomorrow. Cloud Institute International School has flourished day after day, year after year, not only in numbers, but with sheer reputation and recognition for its unmatchable academic excellence and more importantly for the values it has inculcated in the hearts of young learners! We warmly welcome you to explore and experience countless opportunities and endless possibilities Cloud Institute International School offers your child! Cloud Institute also provides online school through RI Smart Academy.
